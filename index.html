01. Grimm's lab webring

A webring is a way of connecting people on the web, popular in the 90s, when individuals with shared interests linked their personal sites together. The sites formed a ring, letting visitors travel from one page to the next — not through algorithms, but through proximity, curiosity, and shared intent.

If the ring is broken, please submit an error report on GitHub .
