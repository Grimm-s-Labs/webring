01. Join the webring

Join Grimm Labs Webring and connect with other members of the community. Follow the steps below to become part of the ring.

02. Criteria to join
  • Be at least somewhat tech focused on your public profiles
  • Not be an asshole
  • Not be racist, sexist, or any kind of bigot
  • Not have an Advertisement-focused site on the webring
It is impossible to enumerate everything that is not allowed. For this reason, we reserve the right to remove a site if we feel it is outside of our own personal bounds.
03. Add yourself to the members list

Add a unique slug, your webpage's name, and your webpage's URL to the members list on GitHub in a pull request.
Pull requests can also be used to change your existing entry or delete your entry.

If you don't have a GitHub account, fill out this form instead:
(ASCII lowercase letters, digits, and hyphen-minuses)



You'll receive an e-mail when your addition/change is made.

If you're editing your existing entry, make sure to use the same slug. If you're deleting your existing entry, leave the URL field blank.
